Your Riding Experience

Welcome to Stable Lilie

Upon arrival, you'll be welcomed with a smile and introduced to your horse. Safety briefing and gear check will be conducted by our friendly staff.

Desert or Beach Trek (Varies by Time)

Begin your ride with a pleasant walk or trot through the peaceful desert landscape. The 3:30 PM departure offers a romantic ride with the stunning sunset as your backdrop.

Swimming with the Horses (The Highlight!)

Head to the water for the main event: the chance to swim alongside your horse in the beautiful Red Sea! A truly unique and unforgettable experience.

Beach Riding & Photo Opportunities

After swimming, enjoy a leisurely ride along the coastline. There will be plenty of time for professional photos to capture your special memories.

Conclusion & Farewell

Return to Stable Lilie, where you can say goodbye to your horse before concluding your 2 or 3-hour adventure.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior riding experience?

No, Stable Lilie welcomes all skill levels! They will match you with a suitable horse and provide a guide to ensure you have a safe and enjoyable ride, whether you're a complete beginner or an expert.

Are there different time slots available?

Yes, you can choose from 10:00 AM, 2:00 PM, or the 3:30 PM sunset ride. The sunset time is highly recommended for the most romantic views!
