THE SACRED PAPYRUS

Must I be a master of the saddle?

Nay, Stable Lilie welcomes souls of all skill levels. You shall be matched with a steed that mirrors your experience, and an instructor will guide your path safely.

At what hours can I begin my quest?

You may choose to start your odyssey at 10:00 AM, 2:00 PM, or the highly recommended 3:30 PM sunset ride — the most romantic hour to behold the desert's glow.

What should a rider carry?

We advise carrying swimwear for the aquatic encounter, comfortable riding trousers, and a heart ready for an unforgettable adventure.
