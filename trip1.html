Trip Itinerary

Hotel Pick-up

Our comfortable transfer service will pick you up from your hotel and drive you to Hurghada Marina.

Arrive at Hurghada Marina

Board the boat and prepare for your day out on the Red Sea.

Boat Departure

The boat sets sail to the first snorkeling location!

First Snorkeling Stop

Dive into the crystal-clear waters for your first look at the stunning coral reefs and marine life, approximately 30 minutes after departure.

Second Snorkeling Stop Near the Island

A second opportunity to swim and snorkel in a beautiful location next to the island.

Island Lunch & Party/Relaxation

Land on the island to enjoy a delicious local lunch and watch a lovely show before relaxing on the beach.

Trip Concludes & Return Journey Begins

Depart the island and begin the 40-minute return trip to Hurghada Marina.

Arrival at Hurghada Marina

Arrive back at the Marina, followed by the transfer back to your hotel.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which islands are available for visiting during the trip?

We typically visit some of the most beautiful islands and areas, including Geziret Hoola Hoola ,Orange Bay, Paradise Island, and other gorgeous stops, depending on the day's conditions and availability.

What should I bring with me on the boat trip?

We highly recommend bringing your swimwear, a towel, sunglasses, and most importantly, high-factor sunscreen/sunblock to protect your skin during the day on the sea.
