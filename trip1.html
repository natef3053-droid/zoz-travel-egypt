The Nomad’s Path

Day I: The Oasis Awakening

Your journey begins with a private sunrise departure towards Bahariya Oasis. Bathe in the sacred sulfur springs, ascend the English Mountain for a panoramic gaze, and wander through the golden dunes of Sandion.

Day II: The Volcanic & Crystal Realm

Traverse the Black Desert's volcanic peaks at El Marsous. Behold the shimmering Crystal Mountain and the wonders of Agabat Valley. Experience the thrill of sandboarding before entering the surreal New White Desert.

The Night of a Thousand Stars

As dusk falls, settle into a royal Bedouin camp. Savor a feast cooked on charcoal, listen to the echoes of nomadic songs, and sleep under a celestial canopy where the stars feel close enough to touch.

Day III: The Ivory Sculptures

Witness a legendary sunrise over the limestone 'Mushrooms' and 'Sphinx-like' formations. Explore the Old White Desert's ancient carvings before your comfortable return voyage across the sands.
