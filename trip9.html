THE SACRED PAPYRUS

Is the path difficult for a traveler?

The voyage is comfortable, yet exploring certain relics—like St. Anthony's Cave—requires climbing ancient stairs. We advise sturdy footwear for your passage through these historic halls.

What attire should a guest prepare?

To honor the sanctity of the monasteries, modest clothing (covering shoulders and knees) is required. Light and breathable fabrics are preferred for your comfort throughout the day.

Are there offerings for the fasting?

Indeed. We provide authentic Oriental meals, and upon your request, special vegetarian or fasting options can be prepared to honor your spiritual practice.
