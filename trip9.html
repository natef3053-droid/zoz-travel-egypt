Trip Program

Hotel Pickup & Departure (5:00 AM)

Our air-conditioned tourist bus will pick you up from your hotel in Hurghada or Cairo (depending on your starting point). Enjoy a complimentary soft drink and mineral water on the way.

Visit St. Anthony's Monastery

Explore the oldest Christian monastery in the world. Your guided tour includes the Old Church, the Holy Cave (St. Anthony's dwelling), the Library, and ancient icons. You will have free time for prayer or photography.

Lunch Break

Enjoy a full lunch meal (Oriental/Eastern food or a fasting/vegetarian option upon request) at a local restaurant near the monasteries.

Visit St. Paul's Monastery

Visit St. Paul's Monastery, known as the Red Sea Monastery. You will see the ancient churches and the cave where St. Paul lived, accompanied by a historical and spiritual explanation from the guide.

Return to Hotel (Approx. 6:00 PM)

Conclude the tour and board the bus for the return journey to your hotel, arriving around sunset (approx. 6:00 PM).

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difficulty level of visiting the caves and churches?

The trip is generally comfortable, but visiting some parts, such as **St. Anthony's Cave**, requires climbing some stairs. It is advisable to wear comfortable shoes and prepare for walking inside the two monasteries.

What should I wear for visiting the monasteries?

It is required to wear **modest clothing** (covering shoulders and knees) when visiting religious sites, in respect of the traditions. Light and comfortable clothing is preferred for a long day trip.
