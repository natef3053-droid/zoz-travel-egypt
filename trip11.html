The Nomad’s Path

Day I: The Awakening & Waterfalls

Your odyssey begins with a royal greeting at your abode. We set forth to the sacred waters of Wadi El Rayan, where waterfalls dance amidst the desert. After a feast for the soul, retire to your sanctuary for a night of tranquility.

Day II: The Lake & Ancient Secrets

Awaken to the whispers of Lake Qarun. Sail across its timeless waters before venturing into Tunis Village, a haven for master potters. The path then leads to the Valley of Whales, where skeletons of giants tell tales of an era before man.

Day III: Sacred Heritage & Green Lands

On the final day, explore the ancient Monasteries and museums that guard Faiyum’s history. Wander through the fertile fields where the earth yields its bounty, before our chariots carry you back to your destination under the sunset.

THE SACRED PAPYRUS

Is the trek suitable for all?

The journey is a blend of comfort and exploration. While most sites are easily reached, the desert walk in the Valley of Whales requires sturdy footwear and a spirit of adventure.

What essence does the Oasis hold?

Faiyum is the land of contrast—where the blue of the lakes meets the gold of the dunes and the emerald green of the fields. It is a sanctuary for peace seekers.

What should a traveler carry?

Bring light linen clothing for the day, a warm wrap for the desert nights, sun protection, and a camera to capture the fleeting beauty of the waterfalls.
