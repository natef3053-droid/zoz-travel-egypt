Trip Program

Hotel Pickup

Our air-conditioned bus will pick you up from your hotel between 9:00 AM and 9:30 AM and take you comfortably to the boat marina.

Boarding the Seascope

Board the Seascope vessel, receive a warm welcome, and a quick explanation of the journey before taking your comfortable seats in the glass-paneled cabin below the sea surface.

Reef Viewing Experience

The boat will sail to the best coral reef area. You will descend into the underwater cabin to watch the beautiful colored fish and corals through the large panoramic windows.

Live Commentary & Photo Time

Listen to the marine guide's explanation about the different species you see. Enjoy free time to take memorable photos inside the cabin or on the boat's upper deck.

Return to Hotel

Conclude the sea trip and board the bus back to your hotel around 12:30 PM, finishing the half-day excursion.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be able to swim or snorkel?

Absolutely not! The Seascope Semi-Submarine is designed to let you view the underwater world from the comfort of an air-conditioned cabin with large panoramic windows. It is perfect for non-swimmers.

What is the best time for this tour?

The morning tour is excellent because it is a half-day trip, concluding around 12:30 PM, leaving you the rest of the afternoon to enjoy your resort or other activities. The earlier time also often provides clearer water conditions.
