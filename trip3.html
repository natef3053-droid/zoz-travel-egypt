Trip Itinerary

Receive safety instructions and embark on an exciting two-hour ride on the quad bike (Beach Buggy) across the desert sands.

4x4 Jeep Ride to Bedouin Village

Switch to a 4x4 Jeep for a thrilling drive to the authentic Bedouin village, where you will enjoy traditional tea and a camel ride.

Desert Sunset Observation

Start the return journey to witness the magical sunset views over the vast desert landscape.

Dinner, Show, and Folklore Evening

Enjoy a delicious dinner and a captivating evening show featuring traditional folk performances and a belly dancer under the moonlight.

Return to Hotel

Transfer back to your hotel in Hurghada.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the recommended attire for the safari trip?

We highly recommend wearing comfortable, old clothes that you don't mind getting dusty. It is also wise to bring closed-toe shoes, sunglasses, and a scarf or mask to protect your face from the sand and sun.

When is the dinner provided during the excursion?

Dinner is provided in the early evening as part of the celebration and folklore show, after the sun has set. It is a traditional meal served at the Bedouin camp.

Do I need prior experience to drive the Quad Bike?

No, you do not need previous experience. Full instructions and safety training will be provided before you start the ride. The activity is generally safe and enjoyable for beginners.
