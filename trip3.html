THE SACRED PAPYRUS

What attire is fit for the sands?

We advise wearing comfortable, humble attire that may embrace the desert dust. Secure shoes, sun-shields (glasses), and a sacred scarf (mask) are essential to protect you from the sun and the winds.

When is the feast served?

The feast begins after the sun has completed its journey. As twilight falls over the Bedouin camp, the folklore celebration and dinner will commence under the desert stars.

Is mastery required for the Quad Bike?

No prior mastery is needed. Our guardians will provide full training and safety scrolls before you begin, ensuring your voyage is both safe and legendary.
