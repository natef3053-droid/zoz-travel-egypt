Trip Itinerary

Hotel Pick-up & Departure

Our representative will collect you from your hotel in Hurghada to begin the journey to Cairo and complete customer gathering.

Police Security Checkpoint

Approximately 30 minutes after departure , there will be a brief stop for the required official security check on the road.

Mid-Way Rest Stop

About one and a half hours later, we will take a quick break at a designated rest area before continuing the journey towards Cairo.

Arrive at the Egyptian Museum

After approximately three hours of travel from the starting point, we arrive to explore the vast collection of ancient artifacts and treasures with your expert guide/translator.

Nile River Cruise

Following the museum visit, enjoy a serene and unique boat trip on the majestic Nile River in the heart of Cairo.

Lunch at a Local Restaurant

Savor a delicious lunch at a local restaurant to refuel for the afternoon sightseeing.

Visit the Pyramids of Giza & Camel Ride

After lunch, we visit the Great Pyramids and the Sphinx, including a memorable camel ride experience.

Begin Return Journey

Start your comfortable bus journey back to Hurghada.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the trip itinerary flexible?

Yes, while the core schedule is organized to ensure you see all major sites, we allocate time for personal enjoyment at key locations. The guide is often flexible with reasonable requests that fit within the overall schedule.

How long is the bus journey from Hurghada to Cairo?

The bus journey usually takes around 6 to 7 hours each way , including a short rest stop for refreshment and comfort. We use modern, air-conditioned coaches for a pleasant ride.

What items do you recommend bringing on this trip?

We highly recommend bringing sunscreen (Sunblock), a comfortable neck pillow for better rest during the long bus journey, a hat/sunglasses, and comfortable walking shoes.
