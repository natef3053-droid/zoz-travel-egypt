Trip Itinerary

Hotel Pick-up & Departure

Our representative will collect you from your hotel in Hurghada to begin the journey to Luxor, followed by customer gathering.

Police Security Checkpoint

Approximately 30 minutes after customer gathering, there will be a brief stop for the required official security check on the road.

Mid-Way Rest Stop (Kilo 85)

About one and a half hours later, we will take a quick break at the designated rest area (Kilo 85) before continuing the journey towards Luxor.

Arrive at Karnak Temple

After approximately two hours of initial travel, we arrive at Luxor to explore the magnificent Karnak Temple, one of the largest religious structures ever built.

Nile River Cruise

Following the temple visit, enjoy a pleasant boat trip across the Nile River, a traditional and enjoyable experience in Luxor.

Lunch at a Local Restaurant

Savor a delicious lunch at a local restaurant to refuel before exploring the West Bank of Luxor.

Visit the Colossi of Memnon

After lunch, we visit the two massive stone statues of Pharaoh Amenhotep III, known as the Colossi of Memnon.

Explore the Valley of the Kings

Continue the tour to explore the burial site of Pharaohs and nobles, including Tutankhamun's tomb.

Visit Hatshepsut Temple

The tour concludes with a visit to the magnificent mortuary temple of Queen Hatshepsut.

Begin Return Journey

Start your comfortable bus journey back to Hurghada estimated arrival at 4:00 PM.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the bus journey from Hurghada to Luxor?

The bus journey usually takes around 4.5 to 5 hours each way, including a short rest stop. We prioritize comfortable, air-conditioned transportation for this route.

Is the trip itinerary flexible?

Yes, while the core sites are scheduled to ensure maximum viewing, we allocate time for personal enjoyment at key locations. Our guide can often accommodate reasonable requests that fit within the day's overall schedule.

What items do you recommend bringing on this trip?

We strongly recommend bringing sunscreen (Sunblock), a comfortable neck pillow for better rest during the long bus journey, a hat/sunglasses, and comfortable walking shoes.
