Trip Itinerary

Hotel Pick-up & Departure

Our representative will pick you up from your hotel in Hurghada to begin your afternoon exploration of the city.

Hurghada Panorama Stop

Enjoy the beautiful panoramic view of Hurghada city and take great souvenir photos.

Visit the Grand Mosque

A visit to one of Hurghada's largest and most impressive religious landmarks for exterior photos and a brief introduction to its architecture.

Sheraton Street & Local Market Exploration

Walk through the old city atmosphere on Sheraton Street, followed by a fascinating look at the Big Fish Market to witness local commerce.

Hurghada Marina Walk

A pleasant walk along the famous tourist marina, enjoying the beautiful sea ambiance, yachts, and the international cafe scene.

Optional Shopping & Dinner Break

Time for optional souvenir shopping or an optional dinner at a local restaurant or Senzo Mall (Dinner is not included in the price).

Return to Hotel

Concluding the City Tour and dropping you off safely at your hotel.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the optional dinner included in the price?

No, the price covers transportation and guiding to all the city's attractions. The stop for dinner is optional, and guests are responsible for the cost of their meal and drinks.

What is the best currency to use for shopping and souvenirs?

While U.S. Dollars ($) and Euros (€) are often accepted, we recommend carrying Egyptian Pounds (EGP) for better rates and easier transactions, especially in the local markets like Sheraton Street.
