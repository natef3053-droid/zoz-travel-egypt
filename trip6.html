The Citizen’s Path

Awakening & Departure

Your urban odyssey begins at 3:00 PM. Our representative greets you at your sanctuary to commence an afternoon dedicated to the secrets of the Red Sea Capital.

The Royal Panorama

Ascend to the city's highest vantage point. Gaze upon the sprawling beauty of Hurghada, where the desert sands meet the infinite blue, capturing memories that defy time.

Spiritual Grandeur

Stand before the majestic Grand Mosque. Marvel at the intricate architecture and towering minarets that guard the city’s spiritual heart under the afternoon sun.

The Market of Wonders

Wander through Sheraton Street and the Big Fish Market. Immerse yourself in the authentic pulse of local commerce and the vibrant scents of traditional Egyptian life.

The Azure Promenade

Stroll along the world-class Hurghada Marina. Admire the fleet of yachts and the sophisticated atmosphere of this international nautical sanctuary.

Optional Twilight Feast

As evening falls, enjoy a moment of freedom for optional shopping or a feast at a local haunt or the Senzo Mall before our final journey.

Return to Sanctuary

At 8:00 PM, we escort you back to your hotel, carrying the glowing lights and stories of Hurghada within your heart.

THE SACRED PAPYRUS

Is the dinner feast included?

While we guide you to the city’s finest culinary spots, the banquet remains an optional offering. Guests may choose their own flavors and fulfill their own desires at their own expense.

What coins should a traveler carry?

Though the realm accepts Euros and Dollars, we advise carrying Egyptian Pounds (EGP) for your tribute at local markets to ensure the most favorable exchange.

What attire is fit for the city?

Comfortable footwear for the promenade and modest attire for visiting the Grand Mosque is highly recommended for all travelers.
