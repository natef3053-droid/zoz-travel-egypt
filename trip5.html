Frequently Asked Questions

What are the available start times for the 3-hour trip?

We typically offer a Morning Slot (starting around 8:00 AM) and a Noon Slot (starting around 10:00 AM). You can specify your preference when you book, and we will confirm the exact time for pick-up from your hotel.

Do I need a driving license or prior experience to ride the Quad Bike?

No, a driving license is not required. The Quad Bikes are automatic and easy to operate. You will receive comprehensive instructions and a safety briefing from our guide before the ride begins.

What should I bring or wear for this short desert adventure?

We strongly advise wearing old, comfortable clothing, long trousers to protect your legs, and closed-toe shoes. Bring sunglasses and a scarf (or purchase one there) to protect yourself from the desert sand and dust.
